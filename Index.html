What is Manoveda?

Manoveda is a futuristic AI-powered wellness platform designed to help you manage your emotions, reduce stress, and feel better — all by simply talking to it. Whether you're happy, sad, stressed, or angry, Manoveda listens and responds to guide you towards peace.

Features

  • 🎧 Personalized mood-based chatbot
  • 🧘 Guided meditations and calming visuals
  • 📊 Mood tracking and emotional journaling
  • 🎯 Stress relief games and relaxing music
  • 💡 Smart suggestions based on your current mood
  • 📈 Weekly wellness summaries and progress charts
